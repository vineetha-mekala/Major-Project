📅 What You Can Do Here

🚀 How It Works


  1. 📅 Each day you visit this page, a log entry is created for you — just once per day.
  2. 🍽️ You can add custom food items anytime, linked to your selected date.
  3. 🔄 You can continue adding meals to already logged dates.


🧮 Nutrient Calculations


Each food entry includes:
⚡ Calories = (Protein × 4) + (Carbs × 4) + (Fat × 9)

These values are added up and displayed as a daily summary.

  • 🥅 Balance your macros according to your goals (weight loss, muscle gain, etc.).
